Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?


My longterm (and shortterm) goal is to help others. In terms of coding, that is using these skills to supplement the work I already do and open up some new opportunities to help assist groups and good causes with these skills.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning


Strengths - have a degree of resilience when things don't go super well. Am curious and want to learn. Can make connections across things quickly.
Limitations - procrastinate and can therefore rush things under time pressure. Can prefer using my gut instinct to figure things out which serves me well most of the time, but in this area and sometimes elsewise having a more systematic approach would be helpful.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?


Greater self-discipline, developing a systematic approach to problem-solving, not getting snappy with people under pressure, working fully cooperatively rather than part-cooperatively and part-competitively.

- What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you


I will do my utmost to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and community representatives. I will seek help from friends who have done the course, and I will also try and summon the courage to ask my facilitators as well.
